# Loan-Portfolio-Risk
Who Borrows, Who Risks, and Where the Money Actually Goes: A Loan Portfolio Story

A credit risk investigation on 30,000+ loan records uncovering where $1B in loans is dangerously concentrated.

This project uses Power BI, Power Query, and DAX to move beyond charts and answer real portfolio risk questions across income, age, gender, geography, and bank type.

🚨 What the Data Revealed
99% of loan value sits with low-income customers ($1.06B)
72% of customers are in one city → geographic concentration risk
Young adult males alone hold $1B in loans
Gender gap in lending is age-driven
12% of bank records unclassified → data quality risk
Portfolio debt rests on $101M/month income base → needs stress testing
